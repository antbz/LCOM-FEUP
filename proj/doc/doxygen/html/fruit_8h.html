void check_colliding_fruit ( FruitBasket *  fruit_basket)

Sprite collision detection between Fruits.

Detects colliding Fruits in a FruitBasket and changes their movement accordingly. Only colisions between whole Fruits that are falling are considered. Two Fruits that have previously collided with other Fruits are not considered so as to avoid weird jittering.
